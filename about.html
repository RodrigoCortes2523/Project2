About Me

I have been playing MMORPG'S since about 2001. The first game I played was called Everquest. Ever since then I have been playing most of these type of games. I think that these types of games are different than other games in that these types of games offer a sense of community where players can play together and interact. Before social media became a big thing these games offered a method for like minded people to interact with each other while simultaneously exploring a large world. With social media being so large today, MMORPG's have lost a lot of interest. A large portion of the playerbase in these games tend to be older. I am one of those players.
