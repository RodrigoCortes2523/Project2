MMORPG
Reviews &
Comments

Massively multiplayer online role-playing games or "MMORPG" are computer games that combine aspects of a role-playing such as The Legend of Zelda or The Elder Scrolls with the massive scale of an online game to create a large world where thousands to millions of players can simultaneously play at the same time.

In this website I will attempt to review some of these games and give some comments on my experience playing them.
